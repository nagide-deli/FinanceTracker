Welcome to Finance Tracker

Your ideal platform for managing your personal finances

At finance tracker, we believe that financial empowerment starts with understanding and controlling your expenses. Our Mission is to provide you with simple yet powerful tools to help you monitor your income, track your expenses, set realistic budgets, and achieve your financial goals.

  • Simplicity and usability
  • Detailed reports and charts
  • From setting savings targets to tracking your financial progress, we help you stay on course to achieve your aspirations.

Features

Discover how finance tracker can simplify your financial life.

  • Income Tracking

    Record and categorize your income sources effortlessly. View consolidated and detailed summaries of your earnings.

  • Expense Management

    Log your daily, weekly, or monthly expenses. Categorize your expenses for better insights into your spending habits.

    Create customizable budgets for different categories,such as groceries, entertainment or utilities. Track your spending against your budgets to ensure you stay on target.
